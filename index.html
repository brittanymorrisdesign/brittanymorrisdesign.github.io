About Me

A graduate from Moore College of Art & Design in Philadelphia, Brittany Morris is an academically trained graphic designer and artist. She is currently working as the lead in-house designer at the University of Pennsylvania School of Nursing. She is currently enrolled at the University of Pennsylvania's 6 month coding bootcamp program, with a goal of becoming a full stack developer.
